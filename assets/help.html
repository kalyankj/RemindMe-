RemindMe@ Help

Here are the hints for using the application:

  • double tap -- on the map to add a reminder at that location
  • tap once -- on the reminder to see the title
  • tap again -- on the reminder to edit, delete the reminder
  • menus -- provides search, list, toggle on/off my location, preference and exit options

Details of the this application can be found at sites.google.com/site/remindmeat. You can also leave comments and thoughts at remindmeat.blogspot.com.

This mobile application provides reminder notification of tasks based on your location obtained through your mobile phone's location sensor. It keeps track of your location and remind any tasks you have in the vicinity which you wanted to be reminded off. Use the previous key or exit to exit the application. Leave the background GPS observer on to be notified of ur reminders when. The background observer service is safe to leave it on and will not consume much battery.
